# Asset Management and Predictive Maintenance Systems

This article folder supports the article:

"Asset Management and Predictive Maintenance Systems: Lifecycle Stewardship and Infrastructure Performance"

It includes reproducible examples for:

- synthetic infrastructure asset registers
- condition scoring
- failure-probability estimation
- criticality-weighted maintenance prioritization
- remaining useful life intuition
- lifecycle-cost comparison
- reliability and risk diagnostics
- SQL metadata for assets, inspections, sensors, work orders, failures, interventions, costs, and governance reviews
- advanced Jupyter notebooks

The examples are educational and synthetic unless otherwise documented.
