# Asset Management Governance Checklist

Recommended governance questions:

1. What assets are included in the register?
2. Who owns asset data quality?
3. How is condition measured?
4. How is criticality defined?
5. How are failure consequences estimated?
6. What maintenance strategies are available?
7. What thresholds trigger intervention?
8. Who approves deferred maintenance?
9. How are lifecycle costs reviewed?
10. How are predictive models validated?
11. How are work orders linked to predictive outputs?
12. How is post-intervention learning captured?
13. What conditions trigger model review?
14. How are service-equity impacts considered?
15. How is residual risk documented?
